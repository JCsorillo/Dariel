About Me

Hey everyone, I'm Dariel Antay, and I'm a student at NISU – Northern Iloilo State University. I'm studying BSIT at the College of Information and Computing Studies (CICS), which is a really cool program! It's basically like being a tech wizard in training, learning all about the latest advancements in software development, data science, and cybersecurity. It's a really exciting field, and I'm constantly learning new things. I've always been fascinated by technology, particularly the way it can be used to solve problems and create new experiences. From a young age, I was drawn to computers and the endless possibilities they offered. This passion led me to pursue BSIT, and I'm excited to be learning about the latest advancements in software development, data science, and cybersecurity. t's a field that's constantly evolving, and I love being a part of it.

But I'm not just a techie – I'm also a huge fan of video games, reading, and writing. There's nothing quite like getting lost in a good story or exploring a new virtual world. Whether it's a fast-paced action game, a complex strategy game, or a heartwarming role-playing game, I find myself drawn to the immersive experiences that video games offer. When I'm not gaming, you can find me with my nose in a book or scribbling away in my notebook. I love reading and writing – it's a great way to relax, escape into different worlds, and let my imagination run wild. And speaking of creativity, I also love taking photos! Capturing moments in time, whether it's a stunning landscape, a candid portrait, or a fleeting moment of beauty, is something I find really rewarding. So, that's me in a nutshell! A student, a gamer, a reader, a writer, and a photographer. I'm always up for trying new things and exploring new interests, so feel free to say hi! I'm always open to meeting new people and learning about their passions.
